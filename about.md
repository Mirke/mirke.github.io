Hello!
 
My name is Mikael Eriksson, (Mirke on Github), I study development and maintenance of software systems at Linnaeus University.
This is not my first time studying on bachelor level, I have taken a courses in linguistics and some natural language processing(NLP) courses.
I was curious about NLP because I wanted to learn more about the techniques of parsing and about machines learning.
 
I really do enjoy programming and one of my dreams is to study artificial intelligence (A.I) in computer science and create intelligent machines.
The fascination for A.I comes from Sci-fi and I do believe that A.I is the next leap for humanity, like the machines were under the Industrial Revolution. 
Yet, I have a way to go. However, I think that the program I am taking right now will give me the steps in the right direction.
